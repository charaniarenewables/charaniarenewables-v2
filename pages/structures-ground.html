Design Process

Soil, Wind & Yield Optimisation

Ground-mount design starts with geotechnical assessment and wind load study. Pile depth, gauge and spacing are determined by soil bearing capacity and wind zone. Panel tilt is optimised for annual yield at the site latitude. Lmaxx supports standard 60, 72 and 144-cell modules in portrait and landscape.
