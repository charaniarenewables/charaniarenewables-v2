Lmaxx Structures

Ground Mounting Systems

For large‑scale solar farms and industrial hybrid projects, Lmaxx ground mounting systems provide the foundation for reliable, long‑term generation. Engineered to withstand high wind speeds and varying soil conditions, our structures support fixed and adjustable tilt configurations.

Request Structural Assessment Talk to Engineer

Engineering Details

Wind Load & Structural Design

Our ground systems are engineered using finite element analysis to meet local wind and seismic requirements, ensuring stability in extreme weather.

Foundation Options

Pile driven, screw and ballast foundations are available to suit varied soil conditions—from soft clay to rocky terrain—minimising ground disturbance.

Corrosion Protection

Hot‑dip galvanising and specialised coatings prevent corrosion and extend structural life, even in coastal or industrial environments.

Adjustable Tilt

Systems can be configured with fixed or adjustable tilt angles to optimise energy yield throughout the year and maximise land utilisation.

Standards & Compliance

Lmaxx ground structures comply with international standards (IEC, Eurocode) and local building codes, simplifying permitting and inspections.

Configurations

Utility‑Scale Solar

Standardised frames for MW‑scale PV farms with single‑row or dual‑row layouts, designed for rapid deployment and reduced BOS costs.

Industrial & Hybrid Plants

Customised support structures for C&I facilities integrating PV arrays with Vertix turbines and MountRex BESS to deliver reliable power.

Adjustable Tilt Arrays

Mechanisms enabling seasonal tilt adjustments to maximise energy capture and improve project economics.

Hybrid Site Integration

  • Engineered for hybrid projects where solar arrays are coupled with battery storage and wind turbines to provide continuous power.
  • Pre‑configured cable trays and equipment pads streamline installation of inverters, combiner boxes and battery enclosures.
  • Our engineering team models land contours and inter‑row spacing to optimise layout, shading and access for maintenance.
