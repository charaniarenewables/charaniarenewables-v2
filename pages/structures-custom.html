Our Process

Fabricated in Pakistan

Lmaxx custom structures are designed by our structural engineering team and fabricated by local certified steel fabricators under our quality supervision. This reduces lead time, lowers cost versus imported structures and supports local industry. All custom structures are supplied with engineer-stamped drawings, material certificates and installation documentation.

Custom Project Process

Custom structures require more upfront engineering. Here is what the process looks like.

  • Concept brief — your requirements, site constraints and aesthetic preferences.
  • Preliminary design and loading calculations.
  • Structural drawings for client and authority approval.
  • Fabrication tender and contractor selection.
  • Construction supervision and quality inspection.
  • As-built documentation and handover.
