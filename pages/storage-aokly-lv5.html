Inverter Compatibility

Works with Your Existing Inverter

The Aokly LV 5 communicates via RS485 and CAN bus and is compatible with all major hybrid inverters installed in Pakistan. No additional interface hardware required for Growatt, Deye, Solis, SolarEdge or Voltronic inverters.

If you have an existing solar system with a compatible inverter, the LV 5 can be added without replacing any generation equipment. We confirm inverter compatibility during the sizing process before any order is placed.
