Generator Technology

Coreless Disc Generator

The GV-3KW uses a coreless disc axial flux PMG — no iron core means no cogging torque, no magnetic drag, and a start torque below 1 N·m. The turbine begins generating at wind speeds where iron-core generators are still stationary. Vibration is eliminated at the source, cutting noise and mechanical stress simultaneously. Service life is rated at 8× longer than conventional motor-type alternators, and the generator can be rebuilt rather than replaced at end of primary life.

  • Start torque <1 N·m — generates in wind that stops conventional turbines
  • No iron-core cogging — vibration-free, <30 dB operation
  • Patent-pending precise coil winding technology
  • 8× longer design life vs conventional motor-type generators
Applications

Where the GV-3KW Is Deployed

Commercial Rooftop

Flat Roof Industrial & Office

IP54 rated for permanent outdoor installation. 3kW output at rated wind speed with cut-in at 2.8 m/s — generating in conditions where horizontal-axis turbines are stationary. Pairs with rooftop solar to produce after sunset.

Ground Mount

Factory Yard & Industrial Estate

8m tower supplied as standard. Civil scope — foundation type and depth — specified to your site conditions. Open exposure and consistent prevailing wind give the GV-3KW its best output profile.

Hybrid Integration

Wind Layer in a BESS-Backed System

The GV-3KW is sized as the wind generation layer in a hybrid architecture alongside solar PV and battery storage. Start torque below 1 N·m means it begins contributing from first available wind — including overnight and overcast periods.
