Solutions

Peak Shaving & Backup

Reduce demand charges and protect against outages with hybrid peak shaving and backup systems. Our solutions optimise tariffs and provide layered redundancy to ensure your operations remain powered through any grid event.

Request Assessment Talk to an Engineer

Use Cases

Tariff Optimisation

Shift energy consumption to off-peak periods and avoid demand charges by dispatching stored energy during tariff spikes.

Load Shedding & Outages

Maintain operations during scheduled and unscheduled grid outages with instant battery backup and automated switchover.

Large Motor Starting

Support inrush currents when starting compressors, pumps and other heavy machinery without tripping breakers or incurring penalties.

Generator Minimisation

Reduce diesel generator runtime and fuel costs by relying on stored energy and renewables for short-term backup and peak shaving.

Power Quality & Stability

Improve voltage, frequency and power factor while smoothing intermittent renewable output and protecting sensitive equipment.

Redundancy Model

Our peak shaving and backup systems employ a multi-layered approach to resilience. Each layer activates sequentially to maintain critical operations and optimise costs.

  • Primary: Grid and renewables supply normal operations with solar and wind offsetting grid consumption.
  • Secondary: Lithium battery storage delivers instantaneous backup, peak shaving and stabilisation for short events.
  • Tertiary: Standby generators and UPS engage for extended outages and provide power to essential and critical loads.
  • Controls: Energy management systems monitor loads, predict peaks and orchestrate resource dispatch in real time.
