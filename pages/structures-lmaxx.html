About Lmaxx

Built for Pakistan

Standard imported structures are often sized for European or Chinese conditions. Lmaxx structures are specified and fabricated locally with materials and dimensions suited to Pakistan’s wind zones, ambient temperatures and typical roof construction methods. Every Lmaxx structure includes structural calculations and an installation manual.

Structural Engineering & Drawings

Every Lmaxx installation is backed by full engineering documentation.

  • Roof loading assessment — dead load, wind uplift and panel weight calculations.
  • Structural drawings issued in DWG and PDF format for building authority submission.
  • Installation method statement and sequence of works.
  • As-built documentation on project completion.
