Structural Systems

Lmaxx Structural Systems

Lmaxx structures provide the critical backbone for hybrid energy installations. Engineered by Charania Renewables, our mounting solutions combine aerospace‑grade aluminium and high‑strength steel to deliver durability, corrosion resistance and wind load tolerance in demanding environments.

Request Structural Assessment Talk to Engineer

Engineering Excellence

As an engineering‑led company, we design every Lmaxx structure to meet stringent mechanical and safety standards. Each system is analysed for site‑specific loads, corrosion exposure and lifecycle performance, ensuring reliability over decades of operation.

Aerospace‑grade Aluminium

We use high‑strength, lightweight alloys for our mounting rails and brackets, reducing structural weight while maintaining rigidity.

Corrosion Resistance

All components undergo galvanising or anodising processes to withstand humidity, salt spray and industrial pollutants.

Wind Load Tolerance

Systems are engineered to meet or exceed international wind standards, giving you confidence in high‑velocity conditions.

Structural Lifespan

Our design philosophy aims for 25+ years of service, matching the lifespan of modern PV modules and turbines.

Compliance & Standards

Lmaxx structures conform to IEC, Eurocode and local building regulations, ensuring safety and permitting compliance.
