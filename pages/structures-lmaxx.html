Why Aluminium

Alloy 6063. No Rust. No Paint. No Maintenance.

Galvanised steel corrodes. In Pakistan’s coastal humidity, industrial air quality and UV intensity, a painted steel structure begins degrading within five years. Alloy 6063 aluminium does not rust. It does not require painting, re-coating or annual maintenance. The anodised surface holds for the 25-year life of the solar panels it supports.

All Lmaxx profiles are extruded and fabricated by Sinopak Extrusion Technologies in Korangi — meaning no import lead time, full dimensional traceability and the ability to cut non-standard section lengths at short notice for complex roof geometries.

Every Lmaxx project is independently specified: roof live load, panel layout, wind zone and seismic zone are all calculated before any materials are ordered.
