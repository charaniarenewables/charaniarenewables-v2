Why MountRex

Our Label. Our Standards. Our Warranty.

Most battery brands sold in Pakistan are sourced from the same Chinese manufacturers, relabelled and distributed with the manufacturer’s warranty process — which means when something goes wrong, you’re navigating an import chain to get a replacement cell. MountRex is different. We specify the product, we set the quality requirements, and we back the warranty directly. If a MountRex system has a cell failure within warranty, you contact Charania Renewables and we resolve it — not the factory, not the distributor, not a third party.

We chose LFP chemistry for MountRex specifically because Karachi’s summer temperatures make NMC a risk. LFP is thermally stable at ambient temperatures that would stress an NMC cell into premature degradation. The choice is not marketing — it is the right specification for Pakistan’s operating environment.
