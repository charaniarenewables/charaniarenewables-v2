About MountRex

Our Own Label. Our Own Standards.

MountRex is the Charania Renewables house label for residential energy storage. Unlike partner-branded products, MountRex is specified, tested and warranted by our own engineering team — tighter quality control at source, consistent documentation, and direct support with no intermediary.
