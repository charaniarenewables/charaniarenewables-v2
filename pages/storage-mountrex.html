BMS

Intelligent Battery Management System

Every MountRex unit has a built-in BMS with complete protection and monitoring. It manages cell voltage, current, temperature and state-of-charge in real time — and communicates all data to your inverter via CAN or RS485.

  • ✓Overcharge, over-discharge, overcurrent and short circuit protection
  • ✓Temperature protection — 4 sensors on battery pack, 1 on BMS board
  • ✓120mA passive cell balancing when voltage difference >40mV
  • ✓SOC and SOH displayed on LCD and reported to inverter
  • ✓Switch-off mode (<300µA), sleep mode and operating mode
