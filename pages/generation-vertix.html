Wind Energy

Vertix Wind Systems

Vertix vertical-axis wind turbines harness low and medium wind speeds with quiet operation and robust engineering. Designed for industrial rooftops, commercial buildings and hybrid installations, these turbines integrate seamlessly into Charania Renewables' hybrid energy architecture.

Request Assessment Talk to Engineer

Key Specifications

Output Range

5-30 kW vertical-axis turbines engineered for small to mid-scale power generation.

Design & Noise

Vertical-axis configuration operates with low noise and minimal vibration, ideal for urban rooftops.

Operating Range

Optimised to capture winds from 3-25 m/s, delivering reliable output across variable conditions.

Construction

Corrosion-resistant materials and engineered blades for long service life and minimal maintenance.

Applications Fit

Designed for industrial rooftops, warehouses, textile facilities and hybrid rural installations.

Typical Applications

Industrial Roofs

Generate on-site power from wind on factory and warehouse rooftops to reduce grid dependence.

Commercial Buildings

Install on shopping malls and office complexes to offset energy costs and enhance sustainability.

Hybrid Rural Sites

Combine wind with solar and storage for resilient power in off-grid or rural community installations.

Textile & Manufacturing

Provide clean supplemental power for textile mills and light manufacturing with vertical turbines.

Hybrid Integration

Vertix wind turbines form one component of Charania Renewables' hybrid energy systems. Pair wind with energy storage, inverters and solar to create resilient, redundant power architectures tailored to your site's needs.

  • Wind energy feeds into BESS for smoothing and storage.
  • Hybrid inverter manages conversion and distribution of energy.
  • Load is supplied with continuous power, backed by grid and storage redundancy.
  • System integrates with solar arrays for optimized generation mix.
