Engineering Process

Every Zetara Project Is Engineered from Load Study to Handover

Zetara HV systems are not off-the-shelf configurations. Every project is engineered around your load data.

  • Load study and tariff analysis to quantify peak shaving savings and backup value.
  • System sizing — discharge duration, round-trip efficiency and degradation modelling.
  • Single-line diagram (SLD) and protection coordination for NEPRA grid connection.
  • Factory acceptance testing (FAT) before shipment.
  • On-site commissioning, grid connection and operator training.
  • EMS pre-configured for your tariff schedule, load profile and peak shaving thresholds.
