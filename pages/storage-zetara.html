Product Overview

Containerised BESS for Industrial Sites

Zetara HV systems are delivered as complete containerised units — pre-wired, pre-tested and ready for rapid deployment. Each system includes integrated thermal management, fire suppression, and a site-specific energy management system (EMS) configured around your load profile and tariff structure.

Engineering Process

Engineering & Commissioning Process

Zetara HV systems are engineered from load study through to handover.

  • Load study and tariff analysis to quantify peak shaving savings.
  • System sizing including discharge duration, round-trip efficiency and degradation modelling.
  • Single-line diagram (SLD) and protection coordination.
  • Factory acceptance testing (FAT) before shipment.
  • On-site commissioning, grid connection and operator training.
