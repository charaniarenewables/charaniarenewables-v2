Inverter Compatibility

Works with Your Existing Inverter

The Aokly LV 10 communicates via RS485 and CAN bus and is compatible with all major hybrid inverters installed in Pakistan. No additional interface hardware required for Growatt, Deye, Solis, SolarEdge or Voltronic inverters.

If you have an existing solar system with a compatible inverter, the Aokly LV 10 can be added without replacing any generation equipment. Our team confirms inverter compatibility during the sizing process before any order is placed.

Warranty & Support

2 Years. Direct Through CR.

All Aokly warranty claims are handled directly by Charania Renewables. No manufacturer referral, no waiting for international responses. If there is a fault within the warranty period, we manage it.

Our channel partners who supply and install Aokly systems are vetted by Charania Renewables and supported with direct technical escalation for any commissioning or in-service issues.
