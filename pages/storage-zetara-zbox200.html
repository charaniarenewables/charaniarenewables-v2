Who It’s For

High-Load Industrial and Commercial Sites

The ZBox200 is specified for sites where a single ZBox100-HS is insufficient — factories with sustained 100+ kW loads, hospital campuses, cold chain facilities and large commercial complexes. The containerised format installs outdoors without consuming internal plant room space.

  • 01 Textile, food processing and multi-shift manufacturing with high MDI demand charges.
  • 02 Hospitals and healthcare facilities requiring sustained backup across all critical loads.
  • 03 Cold chain and logistics where temperature continuity is non-negotiable.
  • 04 Sites needing 2 MWh+ through cabinet paralleling without a full custom system design.
