Our Position

Solar as Part of a System. Not the System Itself.

Most solar companies start with panels. We start with your load. Before any equipment is selected, we model your consumption profile, grid connection, site irradiance and backup requirements. Solar is then sized as one layer — working alongside battery storage, wind generation and grid controls to deliver the outcome you actually need.

  • ✓Tier‑1 modules only — bankable performance warranties, verified supply chain
  • ✓Array sized to your actual consumption data, not a rule-of-thumb estimate
  • ✓Designed for hybrid pairing — BESS, wind and grid interaction modelled from day one
  • ✓Lmaxx mounting structures specified alongside the PV array — one coordinated package

Our Process

Our Solar Engineering Process

Every solar project starts with data — not a catalogue.

  • 01Site assessment: shading, orientation, roof or ground loading and grid connection.
  • 02Irradiance modelling using local weather data and satellite imagery.
  • 03Load profile analysis to size the array and storage correctly.
  • 04Single-line diagram, structural calculations and protection design.
  • 05Procurement, installation, commissioning and handover documentation.
