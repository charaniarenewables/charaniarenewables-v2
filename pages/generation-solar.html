Our Process

Our Solar Engineering Process

Every solar project starts with data — not a catalogue.

  • 01Site assessment: shading, orientation, roof or ground loading and grid connection.
  • 02Irradiance modelling using local weather data and satellite imagery.
  • 03Load profile analysis to size the array and storage correctly.
  • 04Single-line diagram, structural calculations and protection design.
  • 05Procurement, installation, commissioning and handover documentation.
