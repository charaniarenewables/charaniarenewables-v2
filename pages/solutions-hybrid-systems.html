Hybrid Systems

Integrated hybrid systems that blend solar, wind, battery storage and intelligent controls into a single cohesive platform. We engineer systems that harness diverse energy streams to deliver stable, efficient and scalable power for your operations.

Request Assessment Talk to an Engineer

Integrated Architecture

Hybrid systems combine multiple energy sources into a single architecture. Solar and wind generate power, battery storage balances supply and demand, and advanced inverters synchronise everything with the grid or island mode. Structures and foundations ensure long-term stability.

  • Solar delivers predictable daytime generation and offsets grid energy
  • Wind supplies complementary night-time and seasonal energy
  • BESS stores excess energy and ensures seamless backup
  • Smart inverters manage power flow, voltage and frequency
  • Structural systems provide durable mounting for all components

Roles of Each Component

Solar PV

Produces clean daytime energy and reduces reliance on the grid. Solar modules paired with structures optimise tilt and reduce shading losses.

Wind Turbines

Generate energy when wind resources are available, especially at night and during monsoon seasons, complementing solar output.

Battery Storage

Balances intermittency, smooths power delivery, provides peak shaving and ensures back-up power for critical loads.

Inverters & Controls

Synchronise multiple sources, manage power quality, enable islanding and deliver real-time monitoring and optimisation.

Structures

Engineered mounting systems anchor solar and wind components securely, withstand environmental loads and ensure longevity.

Typical Configurations

Off‑Grid & Microgrids

100 kW–1 MW systems supplying islands, campuses and remote facilities with renewable generation and storage for total independence.

Grid‑Connected with Backup

1–5 MW solutions that export power to the grid, shave peaks and maintain essential loads during outages.

Utility‑Scale & C&I Hybrid Plants

5–50 MW hybrid facilities combining large-scale PV, wind farms and containerised BESS, designed for utilities and industrial parks.
