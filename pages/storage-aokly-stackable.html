What’s Inside

Everything in One Unit

Inverter

Integrated hybrid inverter. No separate unit to source, mount or commission. Grid + off-grid + hybrid modes.

High Voltage Box

HV control box manages DC bus, protects battery strings and communicates with inverter and cloud monitoring.

Battery Modules

LFP battery modules, 102.4V / 52Ah per module. 1 or 2 layers. 6,000 cycle life at 80% DoD.

Base

Floor-standing base included. Footprint 653×320mm. Stable, compact installation for residential plant rooms.
