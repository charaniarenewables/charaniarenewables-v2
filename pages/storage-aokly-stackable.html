Why Stackable HV

Flexible High-Voltage Architecture

Unlike fixed-voltage systems, the HV Stackable battery is configured at the point of installation to match your inverter’s exact DC bus voltage. This means you are not locked into a single inverter brand or product line.

  • ✓ Plug connection — no additional DC wiring required between battery and inverter
  • ✓ Extend anytime — add capacity by switching to a higher configuration without replacing the inverter
  • ✓ Mainstream inverter compatible — 204.8V to 512V covers the HV input range of Growatt, Deye, Solis, SolarEdge HV and most major brands
  • ✓ LFP chemistry — no thermal runaway risk, safe for indoor plant rooms and residential installations
