Engineering Process

Every ZBox60 Is Engineered from Load Data

ZBox60 is not an off-the-shelf configuration. We size every system from your 15-minute interval demand data and required backup duration.

  • Load study and tariff analysis — quantify peak shaving savings before any commitment.
  • System sizing — discharge duration, round-trip efficiency and degradation modelling.
  • Single-line diagram (SLD) for NEPRA grid connection approval.
  • Factory acceptance testing before shipment.
  • On-site commissioning and EMS configuration to your tariff schedule.
