Industrial Energy Solutions

Hybrid energy systems engineered for industrial and commercial operations facing grid volatility and rising energy costs. We design resilient systems that combine renewables, storage and advanced controls to cut diesel use and keep your production running.

Request Assessment Talk to an Engineer

Pakistan's Grid Reality

Load Shedding & Blackouts

Frequent outages and brownouts disrupt production schedules, damage equipment and lower productivity.

Tariff Spikes & Peak Charges

Time-of-use tariffs and demand penalties inflate bills during peak hours, impacting profitability.

Generator Dependence

Backup generators are expensive to operate and maintain, produce emissions and create noise pollution.

Power Quality Issues

Voltage fluctuations, harmonics and poor power factor degrade sensitive machinery and reduce lifespan.

Hybrid Architecture

Our industrial systems integrate solar PV, wind turbines, lithium battery storage and grid connection through intelligent controls and inverters. This layered architecture optimises energy flow, absorbs volatility and prioritises critical loads.

  • Solar provides daytime energy and reduces peak demand
  • Wind supplies overnight and seasonal generation
  • Battery storage balances supply and demand, providing backup and peak shaving
  • Advanced inverters synchronise sources, regulate voltage and enable islanding

Financial Modelling & Savings Levers

Peak Shaving

Shift energy consumption away from peak tariff windows by dispatching stored energy and staggering loads.

Diesel Reduction

Reduce diesel generator runtime and fuel spend by leveraging renewable generation and storage for backup.

Uptime & Productivity

Minimise downtime costs by maintaining power during grid failures, protecting equipment and processes.

Tariff Optimisation

Exploit net metering and off-peak rates with smart scheduling and exporting excess renewable energy.

Redundancy & Reliability Layers

Primary Supply

Grid, solar and wind provide the main supply, managed through smart controls and load prioritisation.

Secondary Storage

BESS ensures seamless power during grid events and supports peak shaving and time-shifting.

Tertiary Generation

Standby generators and UPS provide extended backup for essential loads during prolonged outages.

Controls & Monitoring

Supervisory control systems monitor loads, forecast generation and optimise dispatch in real time.

Typical Configurations

Light Industry & Workshops

5–500 kW hybrid systems combining rooftop solar, modular BESS and compact wind turbines for workshops and small factories.

Medium Manufacturing & Warehousing

500 kW–2 MW solutions integrating ground-mount PV, wind arrays and large-scale lithium storage for continuous operations.

Heavy Industrial & Microgrids

2–5 MW hybrid microgrids with multi-MW solar, wind and containerised battery banks powering heavy industry and remote sites.
