Pain Points

Industrial Challenges We Solve

Grid Unreliability

Unplanned Downtime

Production losses from grid outages are quantifiable. We design hybrid backup systems sized around your maximum permissible downtime and critical load.

Peak Demand

Demand Charge Reduction

Industrial tariffs penalise peak demand heavily. BESS discharge during peak periods reduces maximum demand and lowers your monthly electricity bill.

Energy Cost

Cost of Generation

Combining on-site solar and wind with storage can reduce your average cost of energy below grid import tariff for a significant portion of operating hours.

Our Approach

We Start with Your Load, Not a Catalogue

Industrial energy projects fail when they are sold rather than engineered. We begin with your load profile — maximum demand, daily consumption, critical loads and outage tolerance. Only then do we size and specify generation, storage and controls. The result is a system that performs to specification.

Industrial System Layers

A complete industrial hybrid energy system typically includes the following.

  • On-site generation: rooftop solar, ground-mount PV or Vertix wind turbines.
  • Energy storage: Zetara HV BESS sized for peak shaving duration and backup requirements.
  • Hybrid inverter or PCS: manages power flow between generation, storage, load and grid.
  • Energy management system (EMS): optimises dispatch based on tariff, load and generation forecast.
  • Protection and metering: grid protection relay, metering for settlement and monitoring.
