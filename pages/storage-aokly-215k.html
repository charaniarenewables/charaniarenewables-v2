Who It’s For

Large Industrial Sites and Critical Infrastructure

The 215 kWh Container Series is specified for sites where smaller systems cannot meet the peak shaving or backup duration requirement. The containerised format means it can be sited without allocating internal plant room space.

  • 01 Large textile, food processing and manufacturing plants running multi-shift operations.
  • 02 Hospitals and healthcare facilities requiring extended backup across all critical loads.
  • 03 Cold chain and logistics facilities where temperature continuity is non-negotiable.
  • 04 Commercial campuses and large office complexes with significant peak demand charges.
