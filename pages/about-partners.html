About

Our Partners

Our success is built on strong collaborations with tier‑1 manufacturers and engineering firms. We select partners who share our commitment to quality, safety and innovation, enabling us to deliver reliable hybrid energy solutions across Pakistan.

Tier‑1 & Engineering Partners

We work with globally recognised component suppliers and local engineering specialists to ensure that every system we deliver meets international standards and is fully supported throughout its lifecycle.

Aokly

A trusted battery manufacturer providing reliable, high‑cycle lithium batteries. Together we offer residential LV systems backed by comprehensive warranties and local technical support.

zetara

Our C&I battery partner delivering modular high‑voltage BESS racks and containers. Their systems integrate seamlessly into peak shaving and backup architectures.

Tier‑1 PV

We source solar modules from leading tier‑1 manufacturers. Their high‑efficiency panels, warranty coverage and bankable supply chains ensure dependable power generation in our hybrid solutions.

Vertix

Our in‑house wind energy division supplies vertical‑axis turbines engineered for urban and industrial rooftops. Collaboration between our teams ensures full integration with BESS and PV.

Lmaxx

We design and fabricate robust mounting systems for solar, wind and hybrid installations, working closely with structural engineering consultants to meet site‑specific requirements.

Hybrid Inverters

Our selected inverter partners provide advanced hybrid controllers that manage energy flows between PV, wind, storage and the grid, optimising efficiency and uptime.

Why Partnership Matters

Partnerships are at the heart of our engineering approach. By collaborating with component makers, research institutions and EPCs, we can deliver tailored hybrid systems that stand the test of time while simplifying ownership for our clients.

Quality & Compliance

All components we integrate are certified to international standards such as IEC 62619 for batteries, UL 1741 for inverters and ISO 9001 for manufacturing. Rigorous testing and quality audits ensure consistent performance.

Warranty & Support

We consolidate warranty handling, providing a single point of contact for after‑sales support. Our partners back us with long‑term guarantees and access to genuine replacement parts.

Engineering Collaboration

Our engineers work hand‑in‑hand with partner R&D teams to adapt technologies for Pakistan’s climate and grid challenges. This collaboration drives continuous improvement and innovation.

Partner With Us

Interested in joining our network or integrating your products into hybrid projects? Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities and how we can grow together.
