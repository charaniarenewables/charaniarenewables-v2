Selection Guide

Selecting the Right System

The right battery system depends on your daily consumption, backup duration, peak demand, available space and budget. Our engineers run a load study before recommending any product. LV systems suit residential and small commercial loads; HV containerised BESS suit facilities with peak demand charges or high availability requirements.

Battery Chemistry

Chemistry: LFP vs NMC

Both Aokly and MountRex use Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) chemistry. Here is why that matters.

  • LFP is thermally stable — far lower risk of thermal runaway compared to NMC.
  • LFP delivers 3000–6000+ cycles at 80% DoD vs 1000–2000 for NMC.
  • Energy density is lower than NMC but fully acceptable for stationary storage.
  • LFP is better suited to Pakistan’s ambient temperatures and grid cycling patterns.
