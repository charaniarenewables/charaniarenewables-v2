LV or HV

The First Decision That Determines Everything

Voltage class dictates your inverter, installation complexity, available products and total system cost. It is not a minor detail — it is the primary decision in system design.

Low Voltage (48V) — homes and small commercial. Compatible with all major hybrid inverters in Pakistan. 5–30 kWh via standard channel-partner installation. Simple, fast to commission.

High Voltage (500V+) — factories, hospitals and large commercial. Containerised, EMS-managed, site-specific. Requires full engineering engagement: load study, SLD and FAT. 60–215+ kWh.
