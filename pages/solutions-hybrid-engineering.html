Solutions

Hybrid Engineering & Design

Engineering-led hybrid systems design rooted in rigorous feasibility and integration studies. Our HDE team evaluates your energy loads, site constraints and goals to craft the optimal combination of storage, generation, structures and controls.

Request Assessment Book Consultation

What We Do

Feasibility Assessments

We analyse your consumption patterns, load profiles, tariffs and site conditions to determine technical and economic viability of hybrid systems.

Architecture & System Design

Our engineers develop a bespoke hybrid architecture that balances solar, wind, storage and grid interaction to meet reliability and cost objectives.

Integration Blueprint

We translate designs into detailed single-line diagrams, control logic and construction drawings to ensure seamless execution by installers.

ROI & Payback Modelling

Financial models quantify savings from peak shaving, diesel reduction and tariff optimisation, delivering a clear business case.

Regulatory Compliance

Our solutions account for local grid codes, safety standards and permitting requirements to minimise risk and accelerate approvals.

How It Works

Load & Site Data

Gather metered load data, anticipated growth, and physical/site constraints.

Constraints & Objectives

Identify technical, regulatory and financial constraints while clarifying objectives like uptime, cost savings and sustainability.

Hybrid Design & Simulation

Simulate various combinations of generation, storage and grid interaction to determine the optimal hybrid architecture.

Delivery & Commissioning

Provide handover documentation, drawings and support for installers to build and commission the system with confidence.

Deliverables

Sizing & Capacity Models

Detailed kW/kWh sizing of solar, wind and battery components tailored to your load profiles and future growth.

Single-Line Diagrams

Comprehensive electrical diagrams showing how components are interconnected, including controls and protection.

Redundancy & Strategy

Layered backup strategies defining how critical loads remain powered during grid outages or equipment faults.

Financial Models

Breakdowns of capital investment, operating cost savings and payback period under different tariff scenarios.

Implementation Plan

Phased execution plan outlining procurement, construction, commissioning and monitoring milestones.
