Our Philosophy

Engineering First. Products Second.

Most energy system failures are design failures — not product failures. We start every project with a load study and constraint analysis. Only once we understand your load profile, uptime requirements, grid connection and budget do we select technology. Engineer then specify — that is the HED sequence.

HED Project Phases

A typical HED engagement runs through four phases.

  • Phase 1 — Data Collection: load profiles, utility bills, site surveys and resource data.
  • Phase 2 — Feasibility: technology options, sizing scenarios and economics comparison.
  • Phase 3 — Detailed Design: SLD, equipment specs, protection design and grid coordination.
  • Phase 4 — Procurement Support: tender documents, contractor evaluation and commissioning oversight.
