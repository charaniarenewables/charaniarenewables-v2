Sizing Guide

Right-Sized Before Anything Is Ordered

An undersized battery runs flat before the next morning. An oversized one sits half-charged and degrades faster than it should. Charania Renewables sizes every Aokly system from your actual consumption data before recommending a capacity.

  • Calculate average daily consumption in kWh from recent utility bills.
  • Identify critical loads and required backup duration during grid outages.
  • Confirm inverter compatibility and charge/discharge rate limits.
  • Select battery capacity with 20% headroom for depth-of-discharge management.
  • Channel partner handles delivery, installation and commissioning.
