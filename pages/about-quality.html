Commitments

Our Quality Standards

Engineering

Documented Design

Every system is backed by engineering documentation — load calculations, SLDs, structural drawings and commissioning records.

Products

Certified Equipment

We only specify products with relevant international certifications — IEC, UL, CE and local NEQS compliance where required.

Installation

Supervised Commissioning

All installations are commissioned by a Charania Renewables engineer or certified partner — not handed over untested.

Warranty

Warranty: What We Stand Behind

MountRex and Lmaxx products carry Charania Renewables-backed warranties — you deal directly with us. Aokly and Zetara warranties are backed by the respective manufacturers with Charania Renewables acting as the local warranty coordinator. We do not disclaim responsibility — we manage your claim.

Safety

Safety Standards

Safety is a design input, not an afterthought.

  • All electrical designs follow NECA and IEC 60364 wiring standards.
  • Battery systems include cell-level BMS protection, thermal management and fire detection.
  • Structural designs include wind, seismic and dead-load calculations stamped by a registered engineer.
  • Commissioning includes insulation resistance testing, polarity checks and protection relay testing.
  • Operator handover training included on all commercial and industrial projects.
