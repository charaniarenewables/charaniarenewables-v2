About Charania Renewables

Who We Are

Charania Renewables is a hybrid energy integrator based in Karachi.
We deliver engineered storage, generation, structures and hybrid system design for industrial and residential clients across Pakistan.

Our Story

Engineering-Led. Client-First.

We were founded on the belief that Pakistan’s energy challenges require engineering solutions — not sales-led product pushing. Charania Renewables brings together structural engineers, electrical engineers and energy economists to design, specify and deliver hybrid energy systems that perform to specification and stand behind a real warranty.

What We Do

Our Four Verticals

Charania Renewables operates across four integrated business verticals.

  • Storage — LV residential systems (Aokly, MountRex) and HV commercial BESS (Zetara).
  • Generation — Tier-1 solar integration and Vertix vertical-axis wind turbines.
  • Structures — Lmaxx rooftop, ground-mount and custom industrial fabrication.
  • HED — Hybrid Engineering & Design: feasibility, SLD design and financial modelling.
