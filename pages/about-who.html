About Us

Who We Are

Charania Renewables is a homegrown, engineering‑led hybrid energy integrator. Reliable energy, built for your world: we bring together global innovation and local understanding to power a stronger, more resilient Pakistan. Through our exclusive partnership with the Aokly Group, we access Grade‑A LiFePO₄ technology designed for Pakistan’s tough environment, delivering world‑class storage, generation and structural systems.

Our Operating Model

Channel‑Supported Residential

We partner with a network of installers, EPCs and distributors to deliver reliable LV storage and solar packages for homes and small businesses. Our channel programme ensures availability, training and warranty support while maintaining engineering oversight.

Engineering‑Led Industrial

For commercial and industrial clients, our engineers lead the design and integration process—from feasibility studies and load modelling to system architecture and commissioning. We supply high‑voltage storage, wind turbines, structural systems and hybrid controls to deliver resilient, cost‑optimised power.

Mission & Vision
