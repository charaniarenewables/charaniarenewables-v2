Technology

Why Sodium-Ion?

Sodium-ion uses abundant, widely available sodium instead of scarce lithium, removing supply chain risk and reducing long-term cost. Thermal stability is inherently better than NMC and comparable to LFP. Charania Renewables is positioning MountRex Sodium as a forward-looking option for early-adopter commercial and industrial clients.
