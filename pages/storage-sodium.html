MountRex Sodium

Our next‑generation sodium‑ion battery platform offers exceptional temperature resilience and a strong safety profile for Pakistan’s climate. MountRex Sodium brings durable, sustainable energy storage to residential backup and select commercial applications.

Why Sodium?

Temperature Resilience

Sodium‑ion chemistry maintains stable performance across wide temperature ranges, making it ideal for Pakistan’s hot climate and remote installations.

Safety & Stability

Non‑flammable electrolytes and robust cell design reduce thermal runaway risk, offering a safer alternative to traditional lithium chemistries.

Supply Durability

Sodium’s abundant resources mitigate supply constraints and price volatility, enabling long‑term scalability and cost stability.

Intended Applications

Residential Backup

Future small‑scale units designed for home backup, ensuring reliable power during outages with safe chemistry and easy installation.

Commercial & Industrial

Select sodium systems for light commercial and industrial scenarios where temperature resilience and safety are critical.

Hybrid Systems

Integrate sodium storage with solar and wind generation to deliver continuous, resilient hybrid energy solutions across varied conditions.
